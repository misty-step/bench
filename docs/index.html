misty step bench a public benchmark laboratory source

Benchmarks with their homework attached.

Small, sharp, reproducible probes of what language models can actually do. Every score ships with its full method — exact prompts, model and provider configuration, 95% confidence intervals, per-task outputs, and cost — as a single published packet you can audit or reproduce with your own keys.

MIT · specs and results in the open · runs executed through Crucible, which refuses to report a delta it cannot defend.
